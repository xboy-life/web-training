KHANA



khana is a mixture of rice, dal, vegetables and few other items. It is usually eaten at morning and night but also when hungry.It is supposed to be eaten within 10 am.


PANIPURI


Panipuri is a snack made of a round, hollow puri, filled with a mixture.


CHAWAL


Chawal is a type of food made from rice. It is usually eaten with dal and tarkari.


BURGER

Burger is a type of food made from bread, meat and vegetables. It is usually eaten in lunch.


PIZZA

Pizza is a type of food made from bread, cheese and vegetables. It is usually a party food item.
